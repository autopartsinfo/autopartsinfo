Essential Auto Parts and Their Functions

1. Engine Components

The eng ine is the core of a vehicle, consisting of cylinders, pistons, crankshaft, and valves that generate power.

2. Transmission System

Transfers engine power to the wheels through gears, including manual and automatic transmissions.

3. Braking System

Key for safety, the braking system consists of brake pads, discs, calipers, and master cylinders.

4. Suspension System

Includes shock absorbers, struts, and springs that help stabilize the vehicle on rough roads.

5. Spark Plug

Spark Plug ignites the air fuel mixture inside a car's engine cylinder,enabling combunction.

6. Exhaust System

Responsible for reducing emissions and noise, consisting of the muffler, catalytic converter, and exhaust pipes.
